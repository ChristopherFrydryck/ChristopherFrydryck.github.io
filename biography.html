About

Born in 1996 to a loving mother and father, I knew I was always given opportunities to dive into my own interests and find my artistic side. Photography was a major influence as well as music and dance. I always wanted to capture art in its natural form. But I soon found that I never truly knew what was natural.

I am a synesthete. I had no clue what that meant until I was informed that seeing sounds was not normal. Synesthesia, specifically Chromesthesia, is what I was offically reported to have. I experience vivid colors, shapes and textures when a sound, instrument, pitch, timing signature, etc. are played. This gave me an outlook on photography though. I find a lot of my design is highly influenced by the smooth lines and fractures in my vision. If I could replicate it all naturally, I would.

It is generally hard to define myself in a few paragraphs, but I would define myself as curious. I see an opportunity in everything. It's a great outlook on life, because I dont want to miss "that" moment.
