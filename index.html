Hello

My name is Christopher, but most of my friends call me Fred. I am a front-end designer and developer that specializes in UI/UX from Pittsburgh, PA. I plan to use this website to host my portfolio, as well as a great place to contact me.

I am a tinkerer and always will be. You may catch me messing with a Raspberry Pi to modify my graduation cap or building apps for Google Glass, but either way I enjoy it.

Most of my inspiration for my designs are a mix between my synesthesia and my conservation photography. Both are so beautiful and raw in their own way.

For more information, feel welcome to contact me any time.
