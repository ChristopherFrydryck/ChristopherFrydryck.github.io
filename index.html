UPMC Chatbot Research

In September of 2019, I was involved in a two week research and design sprint called "Airplane Mode." The thought was to turn your phone off or on airplane mode in the afternoon and focus solely on research and design to bring back to our team. Unlike Google Venture's Design Sprints, research into the problem had already be addressed by other team members and business stakeholders. It was our job to test with our health plan members how they interact with a Chatbot.
