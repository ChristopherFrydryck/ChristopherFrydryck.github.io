Hello

My name is Christopher, but most of my friends call me Fred. I am a web/app designer and developer from Pittsburgh, PA. I plan to use this website to host my photography and graphic design work, as well as a great place to contact me.

I am a tinkerer and always will be. You may catch me messing with a Raspberry Pi to modify my graduation cap or building apps for Google Glass, but either way I enjoy it. When I am not fiddling with techy stuff, I am either playing guitar, petting dogs or indulging myself in wholesome memes about dogs.

Photography is another muse of mine, especially desert landscapes. I find that getting away from an extroverted lifestyle to spend time with nature is important to me.

“Some of the best designs can be found in White Sands, New Mexico and other natural wonders that have withstood the test of time and human intrusion."

But enough about me. If you want to know more, visit my about section, or contact me and send pictures of dogs (please).
